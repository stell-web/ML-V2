MOBILE LEGENDS

ABOUT MOBILE LEGENDS

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. It was released for Android and iOS devices in 2016. The game features fast-paced 5v5 battles where players control heroes with unique abilities and work together to destroy the enemy's base while defending their own. Mobile Legends has gained immense popularity worldwide, particularly in Southeast Asia, and has a large player base and competitive esports scene.

GAMEPLAY

In Mobile Legends, players choose from a roster of heroes, each with their own unique skills and playstyles. The game is played on a three-lane map, with each lane leading to the enemy's base. Players must work together to defeat enemy heroes, destroy turrets, and ultimately destroy the enemy's base to win the match. The game also features various game modes, including ranked matches, classic matches, and special events.

HEROES

Mobile Legends offers a wide variety of heroes, each with their own unique abilities and roles. Heroes are categorized into different classes, such as Tank, Fighter, Assassin, Mage, Marksman, and Support. Each hero has a distinct playstyle and can be customized with different skins and emotes. Players can choose heroes that suit their preferred playstyle and team composition to maximize their chances of victory.
